Our Story

Lost Friend

The Problem

Every year, over 10 million pets go missing. Without proper identification, most are never reunited with their families. Pet owners face devastating loss and uncertainty.

Finding Hope

Our Solution

PetFinder provides an instant, reliable way for anyone to contact you when they find your lost pet. A simple QR code scan connects finders directly to your information, dramatically increasing reunion rates.

Secure & Private
