Our Story

Lost Friend

The Problem

Every year, over 10 million pets go missing. Without proper identification, most are never reunited with their families. Pet owners face devastating loss and uncertainty.

Finding Hope

Our Solution

PetFinder provides an instant, reliable way for anyone to contact you when they find your lost pet. A simple QR code scan connects finders directly to your information, dramatically increasing reunion rates.

Your Pet's QR Code

This unique QR code contains your pet's information and your contact details. Anyone who scans it will be able to contact you if your pet is found.

What to do next:
  • Download and print the QR code
  • Attach it to your pet's collar or harness
  • Keep a backup copy in your wallet
  • Save the image to your phone
  • Consider laminating it for durability
Pro Tips:
  • Make sure the QR code is clearly visible
  • Use a durable material that won't fade
  • Consider multiple tags (collar + backup)
